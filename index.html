Workflow Health OS

A small visual mock of how Customer Success could turn simple Endex product signals into account health, churn risk, and expansion decisions.

What this is A synthetic example of what CS tracking could look like. It is not a claim about Endex's exact telemetry or product internals.

How to Read This

Status

A plain-English read on the account. Is Endex becoming part of real work, or is usage stuck?

Signal

The observed behavior. For example: exports, repeated similar work, reviewer activity, or repeated corrections.

Next Move

The action CS should take. This should be specific enough that a person can do it the same day.

Status Definitions

Embedded

The customer is using Endex for similar work more than once, exports the work, reuses saved preferences, and has a reviewer involved. This is where expansion can start.

Stalled

The customer is active but not getting to real work. Common signs are no exports, no repeated use, or the same corrections happening again and again.

At Risk

There is some value, but adoption is fragile. The most common version is one strong user with no reviewer or team adoption.

How the System Thinks

1. Observe

Capture basic usage: workbook uploads, generated outputs, citations opened, exports, corrections, reviewers.

2. Infer

Notice when similar work repeats: earnings updates, diligence, reporting, board prep.

3. Score

Grade adoption, trust, saved-preference quality, repeat use, and stakeholder spread.

4. Intervene

Route stalled accounts to concrete CS moves instead of generic check-ins.

5. Expand

Use repeated-use proof to move into adjacent teams, reviewers, or work.

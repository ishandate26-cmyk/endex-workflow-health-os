How to Read This

Status

The account read. Is usage becoming part of real work, or is it stuck before the customer trusts it?

Signal

The behavior behind the read. Exports, repeated similar work, reviewer activity, and repeated corrections all matter.

Next Move

The action CS should take next. It should be concrete enough for someone to do today.

Works Across Finance Segments

Public Markets

Hedge funds and asset managers

Repeated work often shows up around earnings, transcripts, catalysts, coverage lists, and PM updates.

  • Earnings update
  • Merger / regulatory catalyst
  • Portfolio monitoring
Private Markets

PE, corp dev, and deal teams

The pattern is usually diligence, IC prep, model review, and recurring portfolio-company reporting.

  • IC memo support
  • Data-room diligence
  • Operating model review
Corporate Finance

CFO, FP&A, and accounting teams

The stickiness comes from close, reporting, variance explanations, board packs, and recurring forecast work.

  • Monthly close review
  • Budget vs. actuals
  • Board / CFO reporting

Operating Loop

1. Observe

Capture simple usage: files, outputs, citations, exports, corrections, reviewers.

2. Notice

Find repeated pieces inside messy work, not only perfectly repeatable workflows.

3. Save

Turn repeated choices into preferences, templates, source habits, or reviewer formats.

4. Intervene

When work stalls, fix the exact blocker instead of sending a generic check-in.

5. Expand

When similar work repeats and reviewers engage, move to the adjacent team or process.
